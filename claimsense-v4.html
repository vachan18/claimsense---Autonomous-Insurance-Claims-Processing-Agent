Routing Rules

      
How the agent classifies and routes every FNOL claim — applied in strict priority order

    

  

  

    

      

        
PRIORITY 01

        

          
          
          Investigation Flag
        

        
Fraud Keywords Detected

        
If the incident description contains trigger words like fraud, staged, inconsistent, or fabricated, the claim is immediately flagged regardless of damage value.

        
TRIGGER description ∋ {fraud, staged, inconsistent, fabricated}

      

      

        
PRIORITY 02

        

          
          Specialist Queue
        

        
Injury Claims

        
Any claim with Claim Type = Injury routes to a specialist queue for medical assessment and legal review — regardless of the damage amount.

        
TRIGGER claimType === "Injury"

      

      

        
PRIORITY 03

        

          
          Manual Review
        

        
Missing Mandatory Fields

        
If any of the 16 mandatory fields are absent or empty, the claim cannot be auto-processed and is flagged for a human reviewer.

        
TRIGGER missingFields.length > 0

      

      

        
PRIORITY 04

        

          
          Fast-track
        

        
Low-Value Clean Claims

        
All fields present, no fraud indicators, no injury, and estimated damage below ₹25,000 — automatically fast-tracked for same-day settlement.

        
TRIGGER estimatedDamage < 25000
